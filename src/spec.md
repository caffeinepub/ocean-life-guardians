# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create an ocean conservation donation website that educates visitors about marine life threats and allows them to contribute to conservation efforts.

**Planned changes:**
- Build homepage with mission statement about protecting sea animals and marine ecosystems
- Create information section educating about threats to marine life and endangered species
- Implement donation form with preset and custom amount options
- Develop backend actor methods to record and store donation information
- Display showcase of sea animals and marine species with descriptions
- Integrate donation form with backend for submission and confirmation
- Apply ocean-themed visual design with marine-inspired colors (blues, teals, aqua greens) and wave-like elements
- Render generated ocean and marine life images as static assets

**User-visible outcome:** Users can learn about ocean conservation challenges, view marine species that benefit from donations, and submit donations through an engaging ocean-themed website.
